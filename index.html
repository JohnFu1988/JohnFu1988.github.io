Biography

         Dr. Qiang Fu is an Associate Professor at the School of Digital Media and Design Arts, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. He earned Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Department of Computer Science at Beihang University in 2018. He also completed a B.S. degree in Automatics from Beihang University in 2011.

Interests

  • Computer Graphics
  • Virtual Reality
  • Human–Computer Interaction

Research Opportunities

I am constantly looking for new graduate or undergraduate students to join my group! Self-Motivated students with solid math, computer vision, or computer graphics background are encouraged to apply.
